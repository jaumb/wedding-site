How we met

We met through a mutual friend (who is also our officiant) on the Clark University green. Alex swears she doesn't remember this first meeting but Jeff remembers it well even though it was brief. We finished our undergrad degrees and after a few years passed we began hanging out with the same group of mutual friends from Clark. We began dating New Years Day of 2017 and haven't looked back.

Events

The date is the 3rd of July 2021 and we would like you to be a part of the celebration.

Guests Arrive 230PM - 330PM

Hotel check-in starts at 3PM (contact the hotel for early check-in). Shuttle service from the Fairfield Inn & Suites to the venue departs at 330PM. There is also plenty of parking at the venue in case folks want to car pool.

Ceremony 345PM - 430PM

The wedding ceremony will be held in a covered pavillion with open sides.

Cocktail Hour 430PM - 530PM

Cocktail hour will be outdoors, weather permitting, and will include appetizers and drinks at an open bar.

Reception 545PM - 10PM

Reception will be held in the barn and will include dinner, dessert, and an open bar. There will be a fire pit and food truck later in the evening on the outdoor patio. There will be shuttles back to the Fairfield Inn & Suites following the reception.

  Dress code

Keep it casual!

Wear something comfortable as long it's not your birthday suit. Feel free to bring a change of clothes or shoes for dancing if you dress more on the formal side for the ceremony and dinner.

Pandemic

The most important part of the celebration is our family and friends so we want everyone to feel safe while traveling and celebrating. It is possible the celebration will be postponed depending on how the situation develops. We will update this site accordingly as we get more information. At about 90 days prior to the event we will meet with the venue coordinator and decided whether or not to postpone so please check back around that time. We will send email notifications to the address you enter in the RSVP section so please use an address that you regularly check.
